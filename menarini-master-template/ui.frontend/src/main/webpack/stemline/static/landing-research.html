Submission Review Process

CONCEPT

High level summary of the research idea. If in scope, the concept will move on to the proposal stage.

PROPOSAL

Completion and submission of required Stemline Proposal documents (including application form, investigator CV, and a budget, as applicable).If endorsed, a protocol will be requested.

PROTOCOL

For clinical and observational studies, a fully formed, draft protocol must be provided.

STUDY START-UP

Activities such as contracting, final budgets, site feasibility, regulatory review, drug logistics, and other start-up will take place

ACTIVATION & CONDUCT

Study begins and is conducted according to the protocol or project plan, and the contract.

Results & Completion

Final publication or Final Study Report provided to Stemline and study is locally completed by the Sponsor

If you are interested in speaking with someone about your research idea, please contact your regional Medical Science Liaison (MSL) or Medical Advisor, or email us at ists@stemline.com to be directed to the optimal individual or team.
